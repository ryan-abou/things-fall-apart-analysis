Theme: Tradition vs. Change

The novel shows a community rooted in rituals, language, and shared customs. When new religion and colonial rule arrive, those traditions are tested. Some people adapt; others resist. Okonkwo’s refusal to bend collides with a village that is slowly changing, leading to personal tragedy and social fracture.
